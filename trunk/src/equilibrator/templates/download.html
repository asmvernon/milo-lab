Formats

eQuilibrator data is available for download in several formats.

  • JSON: the JavaScript Object Notation format is a lightly structured hierarchical format that is easily parsed in most programming languages.
  • TSV: Tab-Separated Value format is a flat format that is mostly unstructured, easy to parse and easy to view in Excel and other spreadsheet programs. We use the SBTab dialect of TSV which helps provide some structure.

Compound Formation Energies

The standard formation energies of biochemical compounds are available for downlaod in varying pH and ionic strength levels. These can be used to compute reaction equilibrium constants and free energies for various reactant concentrations.

Reaction Energies

The standard change in Gibb's free energies of biochemical reactions are available for downlaod in varying pH and ionic strength levels. Apparent equilibrium constants are also given for those conditions.
